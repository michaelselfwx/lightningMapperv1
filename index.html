Project 1: SPC Outlook & Local Storm Reports Map

Using the ArcGIS API, I have created a very user-friendly map of the most recent SPC Day 1 Convective Outlook as well as all the Local Storm Reports within the last 24 hours. What makes this page even cooler is the users ability to add their own report directly on the page.

Project 2: Weather Balloon Radiosonde Tracking

Using simple software-defined radios (SDRs), I am able to track the launch of weather balloons from all over Florida. With one receiver in the Orlando area and multiple in Gainesville, I have a large footprint of Florida within reach of my hand-built antennas.
